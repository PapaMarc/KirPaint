Kirsten Ruhs

Founder & Director — Art Direction, Themed Finishes

KirPaint Inc., founded in 2010 by visionary artist Kirsten Ruhs, is a premier company specializing in Art Direction and Scenic Paint for Themed Environments. With nearly three decades of in-house experience at Walt Disney Imagineering (WDI) and Universal Creative, Kirsten brings unparalleled expertise to every project.

Key highlights:

  • Global Impact: work spans Disney and Universal parks worldwide.
  • Executive Leadership: led Themed Finishes for high-profile productions.
  • Craftsmanship: mock-ups, samples, models, specialty finishes.

Marc Seinfeld

Director of Business Operations & Shop Foreman

Marc is a technologist and operations lead with decades of systems and product experience. He previously led teams at Microsoft and now focuses on business operations, shop management, and production support for KirPaint.

He holds a B.A. in Economics from Duke University and maintains deep interests in diving, mechanics, and hands-on fabrication.
