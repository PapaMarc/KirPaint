OUR TEAM

KirPaint, Inc. Elevating themed environments through art direction and scenic expertise.

KirPaint Inc., founded in 2010 by visionary artist Kirsten Ruhs, is a premier company specializing in Art Direction and Scenic Paint for Themed Environments.

At KirPaint Inc., we don’t just create environments; we transform them into immersive, captivating experiences. Ms. Ruhs’ leadership and artistic vision drive our success, attracting world-class talent and ensuring unparalleled results.

Kirsten Ruhs

Owner & Sr. Project Development Director — Art Direction, Themed Finishes

With nearly three decades of in-house experience at Walt Disney Imagineering (WDI) and Universal Creative, Kirsten brings unparalleled expertise to every project.


Key highlights:

  • Global Impact: Kirsten’s work spans all six Disney Theme Parks and all five Universal Theme Park locations worldwide. Her commitment to product quality, process excellence, research & development, and documentation ensures creative intent while respecting maintenance concerns for the best long term quality product.
  • Interdisciplinary Leadership: As Sr. Director – Production Art & Design at Universal Creative, Kirsten led the Themed Finishes Team for the Universal Beijing Resort. Her hands-on approach, combined with a strategic view, inspired her team to deliver exceptional results seamlessly uniting all aspects of themed paint, color design, rockwork & facades, and hardscape.
  • Craftsmanship and Vision: Kirsten bridges the gap between concept and execution. Her involvement spans from colorboard phase to installation, maintenance, and refurbishment. She is an expert in mock-ups, samples, models, and specialty finishes.
  • Industry Network: Kirsten maintains extensive contacts across the Theming Industry, including fabrication vendors, individual artists, and maintenance teams at various theme parks. Her global reach ensures access to the best resources and materials.
